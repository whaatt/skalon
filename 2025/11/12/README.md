# hillshade

1. Select your desired location
2. Start with a base elevation map (flat terrain, the actual terrain of your
   chosen city, or a randomly-generated profile)
3. The large sidebar buttons are tools:
   - `⊕`: Pan (just move the map around and zoom)
   - `⛰ ↑`: Terraform Up (click + drag around terrain to terraform upward; zoom
     in for finer drawing control)
   - `⛰ ↓`: Terraform Down (click + drag around terrain to terraform downward;
     zoom in for finer drawing control)
4. Click "Grade My Terrain Accuracy" to receive a grade comparing your elevation
   profile to the actual terrain of your chosen city
5. Click "Share This Map View" to export the current viewport as a PNG
